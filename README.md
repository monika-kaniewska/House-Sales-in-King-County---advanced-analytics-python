# King County House Price Analysis


## Project Summary
This project conducts an advanced exploratory analysis of house prices in King County, Washington. Using a variety of property characteristics, such as living area, land area, number of bedrooms and bathrooms, grade, condition, and spatial information, the study aims to identify the key factors that influence property prices.

## Key Questions

* What are the main property price drivers in King County?

*	How do property features—living area, lot size, bedrooms, bathrooms, floors, grade, and condition—affect prices?

*	Which factors have the least impact on the price?

*	How are the house prices distributed across different locations in King County? 

*	How have prices changed over the years?

## Data
The analysis uses open-source house sales data from May 2014 to May 2015, sourced from Kaggle. It also incorporates geospatial data for King County zip codes, obtained from King County Open Data.
